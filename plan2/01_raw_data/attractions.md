## Conflicting Information
- Chatuchak Market: Confirmed weekends only (Sat-Sun). Some weekday sections exist but limited.
- Lumpini Park hours: Officially 4:30am-9pm per Bangkok Gov site.
- Yaowarat: CLOSED Mondays for street cleaning. Must visit Tue-Sun.
- The One Ratchada: Permanently closed May 2025. Use Jodd Fairs instead.
- Kart Racing: Multiple venues found (E-Gokart, Easykart, SpeedZone). E-Gokart highest rated. Easykart at RCA Plaza more established. Prices vary by session length.
- Vesak Day: May 22, 2026 (Friday). User arrives May 23 (Saturday) - no holiday restrictions.

- TripAdvisor: Review aggregation, user-generated, commercial platform bias

- Forbes & Partners Bangkok Luxury Shopping (Jan 2026): Editorial, may have affiliates
- CK Travels (Jan 2026): Travel blog, detailed and recent
- Bangkok Gov (Nov 2024): Official, highly reliable
- Accor blog (Apr 2026): Hotel group blog, may promote affiliated venues
