## User Preference Note
- Primary transport: **Grab** (fixed pricing, comfort, AC)
- For airport: **Airport Rail Link** (direct, cheap, reliable)
- BTS/MRT acceptable for short hops to nearby malls
- Avoid: Tuk-tuks (tourist trap pricing), unmetered taxis

## Conflicting Information
- BTS fares: Some sources 16-59 THB, others 17-65 THB
- Airport Rail Link Express: Discontinued, all trains now local stops
- Grab pricing: Surge pricing possible during rush hour (7-9am, 5-7pm)
