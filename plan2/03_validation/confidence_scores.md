# Confidence Scores

## Scoring Criteria
- **HIGH**: Verified by 2+ independent sources, one within last 6 months
- **MEDIUM**: Verified by 1 source, or 2+ sources but >1 year old
- **LOW**: Single source, unverified claim, or conflicting information
