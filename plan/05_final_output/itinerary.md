## Practical Information

### Transport
- **Airport transfer**: Airport Rail Link (45 THB, 25-30 min) to Phaya Thai, then BTS to hotel. **With luggage**: Allow 45-60 min total from landing to hotel (immigration 30 min + baggage 15 min + walk 5 min + train 30 min + transfer 10 min).
- **Daily transport**: BTS/MRT ~$4-5/day. Rabbit Card recommended for convenience
- **Apps to download**: Grab (ride-hailing), Bolt (alternative), Google Maps
- **Avoid**: Tuk-tuks for transport (overpriced, use Grab instead)

### Queue Time Summary

### Money-Saving Tips
- Chatuchak: Bargain to 50% off starting price. First sale of day = best deals
- Mall food courts = street food quality in AC (hygiene + comfort)
- Parks are all FREE (Lumpini, Benjakitti, Green Bridge)
- Copper Beyond Buffet: Book via Hungry Hub for best prices, book 2 weeks ahead

### Warnings from Recent Visitors
- **Grand Palace**: "Temple closed" scam common - ignore anyone saying it's closed. Check official hours yourself.
- **Tuk-tuks**: Always negotiate price first. Better to use Grab for fixed pricing.
- **Chatuchak**: Bring cash (most stalls don't accept cards). Stay hydrated (buy coconut water every 45 min).
- **Yaowarat**: Closed Mondays for street cleaning - plan accordingly.
- **Heat**: 32-35°C in May. Plan indoor activities 12pm-4pm.

### Dress Code
- **Grand Palace**: Strict! Shoulders + knees covered. No shorts, no sleeveless. Rent clothes at entrance if needed (200 THB).
- **Temples**: Remove shoes before entering. Modest clothing required.

### Emergency Contacts
- Tourist Police: 1155 (toll-free, English-speaking)
- Emergency: 191
- US Embassy: +66-2-205-4000
