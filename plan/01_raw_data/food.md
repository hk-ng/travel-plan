## Conflicting Information
- Sukhumvit Soi 38: Some sources say it's been reduced due to city cleanup (2023+)
- Jay Fai: Long queues (3+ hours), some say not worth the wait
- Mall food courts vs street food: Locals say mall food courts are "street food in AC" - good hygiene alternative
