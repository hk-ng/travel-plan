- Avg travel time: 15-20 min by MRT/BTS
- Transport mode: MRT + walking
- Best for: Saturday/Tuesday evenings (Yaowarat closed Monday)

## Optimization Decisions

### Decision 1: Chatuchak on Sunday (May 24)
- Alternatives considered: Skip Chatuchak, visit weekday plant section only
- Why Sunday won: Only day full market is open during trip. Highest shopping + food score.
- Trade-offs: Requires early start (9am) despite user preference for late starts. Compromise: leave hotel at 10am, arrive 10:30am (still good, beat peak heat).

### Decision 2: Copper Beyond Buffet on Saturday (May 23) evening
- Alternatives considered: Sunday or Monday
- Why Saturday won: Arrival day evening activity. Near hotel area (Siam/Chit Lom). User's top preference.
- Trade-offs: None - perfect fit for arrival evening.

### Decision 3: Monday (May 25) = Indoor/Mall day
- Alternatives considered: Outdoor temples, parks
- Why indoor won: Chatuchak CLOSED, Yaowarat CLOSED. Hot season + afternoon rain risk.
- Trade-offs: Less cultural experience, but optimal for weather and availability.

### Decision 4: Grand Palace on Tuesday morning (May 26)
- Alternatives considered: Sunday morning, Saturday afternoon
- Why Tuesday won: Sunday reserved for Chatuchak. Saturday arrival too late. Tuesday morning before departure is optimal.
- Trade-offs: Must leave early for flight. Compromise: visit Grand Palace 8:30-11am, then airport.

### Decision 5: Hotel area = Siam/Chit Lom
- Alternatives considered: Sukhumvit, Silom, Riverside
- Why Siam won: Highest food access score (Copper Beyond Buffet, Siam Paragon, CentralWorld all walkable). Best BTS hub for all clusters.
- Trade-offs: Slightly more expensive than Sukhumvit, but worth it for food/shopping proximity.
