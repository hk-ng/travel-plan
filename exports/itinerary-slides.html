Important Warnings

Grand Palace scam: "Temple closed" - ignore anyone saying this. Check official hours yourself.
Tuk-tuks: Always negotiate price first. Better to use Grab for fixed pricing.
Chatuchak: Bring cash (most stalls don't accept cards). Stay hydrated (buy coconut water every 45 min).
Yaowarat: Closed Mondays for street cleaning - plan accordingly.
Heat: 32-35°C in May. Indoor activities planned 12pm-4pm.
